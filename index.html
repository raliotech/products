1. Introduction

Welcome to the Ralio Link App. We prioritize protecting your privacy and personal information. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard the data you provide while using our application (app). By using the Ralio Link App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Data Collection and Usage

  • The app requires access to your device's microphone to enable voice command functionality, allowing you to control your robot using simple voice commands such as "forward," "backward," and more.
  • All voice inputs are processed locally on your device to execute these commands efficiently and securely.
  • We do not collect, record, store, or transmit any audio data to external servers or third parties.

3. Data Security

  • Your data security is our priority. Voice data is processed exclusively on your device, ensuring that it remains private and secure. Local processing minimizes exposure to external threats, as no data is transmitted over networks or to external servers, enhancing both user privacy and overall security.
  • We employ industry-standard security measures provided by the core functionality of the operating system (iOS, Android) to protect the integrity and confidentiality of your data during app usage. These measures do not involve third-party services or custom solutions.
  • The app does not share or transmit any data outside your device, further reducing potential vulnerabilities.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We value your trust and do not share, sell, or disclose your data to any third-party entities. Your information stays with you, is never used for advertising or other commercial purposes, and is not monetized through analytics or other methods.

Any updates will be posted on this page, and the "Effective Date" will be revised accordingly. We encourage you to review this policy regularly to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.
